Describing My Project

My project idea is going to be a table about Pokemon collections. Now what I have in mind is just a simple way for users to keep track of what kind of cards they have in their collection. For example once they obtain a card in real life and they want to keep that card then they will open my site and add the information of that card into their table. And obviously users will be able to delete and edit their collection.

As stated by the requirements to keep collections private, every trainer will create a secure account with a password so only they can manage their specific cards.

If users use my site correctly then they would not have to go through their physical binders to look for a specific card they want to make sure they have. They can just pull up my site and search or sort through thier collection easily.
